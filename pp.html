Privacy Policy

Welcome to Immortal Library. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights while using the website.

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Visitors

Immortal Library does not require visitors to create an account or log in.

We do not intentionally collect, store, or process personal information from normal visitors.

Examples include

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • Payment Information
  • Login Credentials

Visitors can browse and read publicly available content without providing personal information.

2. Administrator Information

A limited number of authorized administrators may sign in to manage Immortal Library.

Information stored for administrators may include

  • Email Address
  • Display Name
  • Profile Picture
  • Administrator Role
  • Information required to manage uploaded content

This information is used solely to secure administrator access and manage website content.

3. Uploaded Content

EPUB files, book covers, metadata, and related information uploaded by administrators are stored to operate Immortal Library.

These files are considered website content and are not personal visitor information.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Immortal Library does not intentionally use cookies or tracking technologies to identify or profile normal visitors.

Third-party services such as hosting providers, authentication providers, browsers, or content delivery services may use their own cookies or similar technologies.

5. Third-Party Services

Immortal Library may use trusted third-party services for hosting, authentication, databases, cloud storage, file delivery, and related infrastructure.

These providers process information only as necessary to provide their services and are governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect administrator information and website data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

No internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Immortal Library is not specifically directed toward children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Since Immortal Library does not maintain accounts or personal information for normal visitors, there is generally no personal data for visitors to access, modify, or delete.

Administrators may request updates or removal of their stored administrative information where applicable.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated, revised, or modified at any time without prior notice.

Continued use of Immortal Library after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the administrator through the official contact methods provided by Immortal Library.

Acknowledgement

By using Immortal Library, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
