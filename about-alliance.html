Alliance Information

Heaven And Earth Great Love Alliance

Heaven And Earth Great Love Alliance is the headquarters of the alliance. Inspired by Reverend Insanity, it is home to readers who enjoy discussing its characters, philosophies, cultivation paths, theories, lore, memes, and countless "What If?" scenarios.

Highlights

  • Main headquarters of the alliance.
  • Community inspired by Reverend Insanity.
  • Discussions, theories, lore, and philosophy.
  • Cultivation-related conversations.
  • Community events and collaborations.

Immortal Court

Immortal Court is the official development community of Immortal Library. It serves as the home of ongoing projects created by Febris GG, including novels, software, websites, and future creative works.

Members can follow development progress, receive previews, participate in discussions, provide feedback, and witness ideas gradually becoming reality.

Highlights

  • Official Immortal Library development server.
  • Development updates and announcements.
  • Original novels and coding projects.
  • Community feedback and testing.
  • Behind-the-scenes development.
  • Home of the developer.

Fire Zone

Fire Zone is an all-purpose fiction community dedicated to bringing together fans from every corner of fictional media, whether it be anime, manga, novels, web novels, visual novels, games, or films.

Highlights

  • General fiction community.
  • Animanga discussions.
  • Novels and web novels.
  • Visual novels and games.
  • Recommendations and community events.
  • Casual conversations and entertainment.

Alliance Principles

  • Every server maintains its own unique identity.
  • Members are welcome across every allied community.
  • Collaboration is encouraged over competition.
  • Respect every community and its leadership.
  • Support creators, readers, and original works.
  • Grow together while preserving each community's individuality.

Roles Within the Alliance

Although each community remains independent, every server fulfills a unique responsibility within the alliance.

Heaven And Earth Great Love Alliance

  • Headquarters of the alliance.
  • Central community connecting all allied servers.
  • Main source of EPUB preservation and distribution.
  • Primary hub for alliance announcements and collaborations.
  • Focused on the Reverend Insanity fandom and cultivation discussions.

Immortal Court

  • Official development branch of the alliance.
  • Responsible for the development and maintenance of Immortal Library.
  • Develops and maintains the website, backend services, and future projects.
  • Oversees technical infrastructure and long-term development.
  • Works alongside allied communities to improve the library.

Fire Zone

  • Official allied community of the alliance.
  • Broad fiction community welcoming readers of every genre.
  • Contributes to EPUB preservation and sharing.
  • Provides Visual Novel and fiction-related EPUBs.
  • Supports cross-community events and collaborations.
